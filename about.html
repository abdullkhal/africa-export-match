About Africa Export Match

Africa Export Match connects global buyers with vetted African exporters. We exist to reduce friction, shorten sourcing cycles, and help great suppliers reach international markets.

Our Story

We started after seeing buyers struggle to find reliable partners and suppliers struggle to reach the right demand. Our team combines trade, compliance and logistics experience across West, East and Southern Africa.
