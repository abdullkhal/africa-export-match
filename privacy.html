Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Africa Export Match collects and uses personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling data in a transparent way.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details (name, email, company, country) submitted via forms.
  • Business information (product needs, capacities, certifications) you choose to provide.
  • Usage data from cookies/analytics to improve the website.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide introductions.
  • To verify suppliers and match them with buyer requests.
  • To improve our services and website experience.

Legal Bases

Where applicable, we rely on legitimate interest, consent (for marketing), and contract necessity.

Sharing

We share information only as needed to provide the Services (e.g., sharing a buyer brief with a potential supplier) or where required by law. We do not sell personal data.

Cookies & Analytics

We use basic analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand site usage. You can block cookies in your browser settings.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including access controls and encrypted transport (HTTPS).

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law.

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)

International Transfers

Where data is transferred across borders, we use appropriate safeguards.

Contact

To exercise rights or make requests, contact info@africaexportmatch.com.
